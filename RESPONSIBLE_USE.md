# Responsible Use

`CUDA_Mnemonic_Recovery` is intended for legitimate mnemonic recovery and audit workflows.

Use it only when you have a lawful right to access the wallet, seed phrase, or recovery material involved.

Examples of acceptable use:

- restoring your own incomplete backup phrase
- disaster recovery for wallets you control
- recovery work explicitly authorized by the owner
- lab or test-environment validation

Examples of unacceptable use:

- attempting to recover phrases for wallets you do not own
- unauthorized access to third-party funds or accounts
- any workflow that violates local law, policy, or contractual obligations

The author strongly discourages any malicious use of this software. Any unlawful, abusive, or unauthorized use is entirely the responsibility of the user.

The maintainer and contributors do not endorse misuse of this software.
